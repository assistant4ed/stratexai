Section 1

Executive Summary

TechCorp HK operates at the intersection of enterprise SaaS and AI-driven workflow automation in the Hong Kong and Greater Bay Area market. Based on our analysis, the addressable market for AI-assisted business process automation in the region is estimated at HK$4.2 billion (2025), growing at a compound annual rate of 18.4% through 2028, driven by accelerating digital transformation initiatives across financial services, logistics, and professional services sectors.

Our competitive landscape assessment identified five primary competitors with meaningful market share. TechCorp HK holds a differentiated position through its proprietary integration layer and Cantonese-first NLP capabilities — a structural moat that global competitors have not yet replicated. However, pricing pressure from well-funded regional entrants and a nascent enterprise sales motion represent the most material near-term risks to market share expansion.

We recommend a three-pronged strategic response: (1) accelerate enterprise channel partnerships with the top-four accounting and consulting firms operating in the GBA; (2) introduce a mid-market product tier at HK$2,800/month to close the current gap in the competitive funnel; and (3) pursue a Series A fundraise anchored by the Hong Kong Investment Corporation's Technology Fund, targeting a close within Q3 2026.
